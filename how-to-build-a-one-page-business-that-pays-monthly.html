How to Build a One-Page Business (That Pays Monthly)

Most business plans are bloated. The simplest model that works? A one-page product that people can buy — now.

Here’s what you need:

1. Product
  • What: Pick a clear result (e.g. “Notion setup for freelancers”)
  • Price: Keep it under $500 to start
2. Page
  • Use Carrd or Notion: 1-page layout with offer, testimonials, FAQ
3. Payment Option
  • Use Gumroad or Stripe: Collect payments easily

Launch with zero-code. Improve based on feedback. Once it sells, add automation (onboarding email, delivery system, upsells).
