Design Without Photoshop

Great design shouldn't be gatekept by pro software. These tools make it easy for creators, marketers, and founders to create stunning visuals—no Photoshop required.

  1. Canva Pro – Templates for Everything
    Thumbnails, social posts, pitch decks—drag, drop, done.

  2. Figma – Collaborative UI Design
    Create landing pages, mockups, or dashboards with real-time collaboration.

  3. Lunacy – Free Design App With Built-in Assets
    Tons of icons, photos, and illustrations built right in. No internet needed.

  4. Remove.bg – One-Click Background Removal
    Turn basic images into polished assets instantly.

  5. Khroma – Smart Color Palette Generator
    Find consistent colors that actually look good together.

Bonus Tip: Use Canva’s Brand Kit to instantly apply your colors and fonts across designs.
