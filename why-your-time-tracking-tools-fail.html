Why Your Time Tracking Tools Fail (and What to Use Instead)

You're not broken. Your tool is.

Many freelancers and solopreneurs blame themselves when they fail to stick to a time tracking habit. But here's the truth: most tools are built for teams, not individuals.


Common Issues:

  • Too many manual steps to log time
  • Ugly, cluttered interfaces
  • No feedback loop or reward for consistency

Better Alternatives:

  • Toggl Track – Clean UI and blazing fast tracking
  • RescueTime – Works automatically in the background
  • TimeCamp – Tracks billable hours + integrates with Trello/Asana


Pro Tip: Block 5 minutes every Friday to review your weekly dashboard. Small tweaks = massive gains.
